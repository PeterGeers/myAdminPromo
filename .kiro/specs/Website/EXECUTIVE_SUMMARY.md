# myAdmin SaaS Website - Executive Summary

**Last Updated:** March 2026  
**Status:** Planning Phase  
**Reading Time:** 5 minutes

## What is myAdmin?

myAdmin is a **Financial Administration Platform with Integrated STR Management** designed for small businesses, accountants, and property managers who need intelligent automation.

## Strategic Decision: STR-Focused Website

**Initial Website Strategy: 100% Focus on STR Property Managers**

**Platform Architecture:**

- Core: Financial administration (invoices, banking, taxes, reports)
- First Module: STR analytics and pricing optimization
- Future: More domain modules (but NOT mentioned on this website)

**Market Strategy for Initial Website:**

- **ONLY Target**: STR property managers (3-15 properties)
- **Value Proposition**: One platform replaces PMS + accounting software + spreadsheets
- **Key Message**: Manage rentals + Optimize pricing + Handle taxes
- **No Mention Of**: General businesses, modular approach, future modules

**Future Strategy (Separate Website):**

- Later, we'll create a different website for general businesses
- That site will focus on financial administration with modular add-ons
- Different messaging, different target audience, different positioning

**Why This Single-Focus Approach Works:**

- Clear, focused message (no confusion about who we serve)
- STR property managers see themselves immediately ("this is for me")
- Solves their specific pain: juggling multiple tools
- Honest positioning: we're the integrated solution for STR + financials
- Easier to market, easier to convert

- Time-consuming admin work

**Solution:**

- Data flows seamlessly from bookings → financials → tax declarations
- AI-powered pricing insights based on your historical data
- One platform replaces 3-4 tools

**Value:**

- Stop juggling multiple tools
- Automated data flow (no manual entry)

- More time for guests, less time on spreadsheets

## Feature Hierarchy

### For STR Property Managers (ONLY Target)

**Integrated Solution - Two Pillars of Equal Importance:**

**Pillar 1: STR Analytics & Pricing**

- Multi-platform integration (Airbnb, Booking.com)
- AI-powered pricing recommendations (based on your historical data)
- Revenue analytics and reporting (year-month matrix, channel performance)
- Booking management (realized vs planned)
- Guest analytics (returning guests, country reports)
- Occupancy tracking and ADR analysis

**Pillar 2: Financial Administration**

- AI-powered invoice processing (universal extraction, any format)
- Automated banking (CSV import, pattern recognition, auto-categorization)
- Tax compliance (VAT/BTW, Income Tax/IB, Tourist Tax)
- Financial reports (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- Document management (Google Drive integration)
- Audit trail for compliance

**The Integration Advantage:**
Data flows seamlessly: Bookings → Revenue → Expenses → Financial Reports → Tax Declarations
One platform, one login, zero manual data entry between systems.
